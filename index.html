How does it work?

Restaurants create pick-up requests that volunteers can accept. Each pick-up contains left over food and gets delivered to an organization that can put it to good use.

Improving together.

While all of the pick-ups are volunteer work, users are able to track their delivery statistics and share their stats with others. Users can also earn achievements based on milestones and the amount of time spent using Replate.

45 million Americans rely on stipends to buy food each month. It's time to make a difference.
